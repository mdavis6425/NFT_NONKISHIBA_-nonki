FAQ

What is an NFT?

It stands for non-fungible token, which is a fancy way of saying it’s a unique, one-of-a-kind digital item that users can buy, own, and trade.

What is “Mint” or “Minting”?

“Mint” or “minting” is how your digital art becomes a part of the Ethereum blockchain - a public ledger that is unchangeable and tamper-proof. Your digital artwork is represented as an NFT so it can then be purchased and traded in the market where it is digitally tracked as it is resold or collected again in the future.

How do I mint a Nonki Shiba NFT?

First you need to have Metamask installed. It is a crypto wallet that is super easy to use and you can learn more about it here. After that, you will need some ETH. You can buy it on Coinbase or even directly from your Metamask wallet.

When can I mint?

Presale is on Dec 3rd from 2am to 10pm UTC. Official launch is on Dec 3rd at 11pm UTC.

How much does a Nonki Shiba cost?

They are priced at 0.05 ETH during presale and 0.06 ETH during public sale.

Where does my NFT go after I mint one?

Your NFT will appear in your Ethereum address, or the connected Metamask wallet you used to mint. You can also view them on OpenSea.

Are the NFTs securely stored?

The digital art and metadata are saved on IPFS and the smart contract will keep track of both.

Is there any plans on utilities for Nonki Shiba in the future?

Yes, the immediate plan after the launch is to add add utility token. NFT holders can earn certain amount of token daily for each NFT they own. This can be used to trade for merch and rarity ticket. Rarity ticket is used to mint a new collection called "Companion Shiba" for free. Breeding will also be introduced so basically, Nonki Shiba + Companion Shiba = Kid Shiba.
