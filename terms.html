Every transaction on the Nonki Shiba Platform is subject to Fees collected to support the creators, Nonki Shiba Platform and Nonki Studios.

The amount of Fees for the sale of a Nonki Shiba Item also depends on whether the transaction involves the Nonki Shiba Item’s first sale (“Primary Sale”) or a succeeding sale in the secondary market (“Secondary Market Sale”).  All Fees are collected and distributed at the time of the purchase of a Nonki Shiba Item by operation of the Smart Contracts.

Primary Sale

Secondary Market Sale
